How Alfred Works

Think of Alfred as a smart assistant with a toolbox. When you ask for something, Alfred's AI brain figures out the best tool for the job. It can search the web, generate an image, or just chat. Beyond simply picking a tool, Alfred also taps into its long-term memory of your past conversations to give you smarter, more relevant answers. It learns from your conversations to give you a more personalized experience over time.

Base AI Models

Alfred's intelligence is powered by different base models tailored to your needs. You can switch between these models in /user-settings.

  • Gemma (Default): The standard model for free tier users, offering high-quality reasoning.
  • Gemini (Subscribers): Our most advanced model, reserved for Supporter and Pro tiers. Gemini offers superior reasoning, better accuracy, faster response times, and advanced multi-modal capabilities.

Getting Started in 2 Easy Steps

1. Add Alfred to Your Server

Click the "Add to Discord" button on our website or in Alfred's profile. You'll be asked to authorize it to join your server.

2. Start Chatting!

There are a few ways to talk to Alfred:

  • Mention: Simply @AlfredHelps in any channel.
  • Trigger Word: Just saying "alfred" in a message will get its attention (server admins can change this).
  • Slash Commands: Use commands like /ask for direct questions. This is the best way to use Alfred in DMs.

Core Features & Commands

Alfred's Long-Term Memory (RAG)

Ever feel like you have to repeat yourself to a bot? Not with Alfred. He uses a special system called Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to remember your past conversations. When you chat, Alfred intelligently searches your history for relevant context and uses it to provide smarter, more personalized answers. It's like having a conversation with a friend who actually remembers what you've talked about.

  • Automatic Context: Alfred automatically uses this memory in every conversation.

AI & Conversation

  • Chat with Alfred: Use /ask or mention Alfred to start a conversation. You can even attach images for Alfred to analyze.
  • AI-Generated Profiles: Use /overview to see an AI-generated summary of your personality and chat habits.
  • Check Your Vibe: Use /compatibility [user] to see an AI-powered analysis of how well you and another user get along based on your chat styles.

Image Magic

  • Generate Images: Use /imagine with a descriptive prompt to create AI-generated images in various styles.
  • Reimagine Existing Images: Right-click on any image and select `Apps > Reimagine`. Alfred will create a new image inspired by the original.
  • Describe What You See: Right-click an image and use `Apps > Describe` to get a detailed text description of the picture.
  • Upscale Images (Pro): Use /image-upscale to increase the resolution and quality of an image.

Productivity & Tools

  • Deep Research: Use /deep-research [topic] for a detailed report on any subject. Alfred will browse the web and summarize its findings for you.
  • Smart Reminders: Never miss a task again. Simply ask Alfred to "Remind me to [task] in [time]" and he will send you a DM when the time is up.
  • YouTube & Media: Alfred can search YouTube for you and fetch information about specific videos. He can also process audio and video attachments to answer questions about their content. (Note: Alfred cannot "watch" YouTube videos directly via link).
  • Summarize Anything: Right-click on a long message or an image and use `Apps > Summarize` to get a quick summary.

Personalization & Profiles

  • User Settings: The /user-settings command is your central hub. Here you can:
    • Pick your preferred AI model.
    • Switch between "Casual" and "Serious" conversation modes.
    • Manage your privacy settings.
  • Custom Personalities (Pro): Subscribers can build their own AI assistants.
    • /create-personality: Design a new AI with a unique name, instructions, and behavior.
    • /list-personalities: View all the custom AIs you've created.
    • /delete-personality: Remove a custom AI you no longer need.
  • Your Profile: Use /profile to see your stats, badges, and rank.
  • Notifications: Use /notifications to see updates from the developers and system messages.

Your Data, Your Control

You have full control over your data.

  • Personalization Opt-Out: In /user-settings > `Privacy Settings`, you can turn off the message analysis that Alfred uses to learn your style and generate your /overview.
  • Delete Your History: Use /delete-history to clear Alfred's memory in the current channel. Use /delete-all-history to permanently erase all of your stored messages everywhere. This also deletes your embedding data for the RAG system.
  • Export Your Data: Use /export-history to get a copy of your chat logs in the current channel.

For Server Admins

Admins with "Manage Server" permissions can use /server-settings to customize Alfred:

  • Family Friendly Mode: Keep conversations clean by restricting the bot to a family-friendly AI.
  • Custom Trigger Word: Change the word that activates Alfred (the default is "alfred").
  • General Chatter: Allow Alfred to occasionally join in on conversations to make the server more lively.

Troubleshooting & FAQ

Having trouble? Here are some common questions and answers.

  • Why isn't Alfred responding?
    • Make sure Alfred has permission to read and send messages in the channel.
    • Check the server's trigger word in /server-settings.
    • You might be on a cooldown for sending messages too quickly.
  • Why did I get an "interaction failed" error? This usually means Alfred is thinking and took a little too long to respond. Try your request again in a few moments.
  • How do I use the right-click commands (like Summarize or Reimagine)? On desktop, right-click a message or image. On mobile, tap and hold the message, then find the "Apps" option in the menu that appears.
