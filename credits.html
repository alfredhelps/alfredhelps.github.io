Credits & Subscriptions

Power up your AlfredHelps experience with our flexible monetization models.

How It Works

We offer a simple and transparent system to access Alfred's premium features.

The Credit System

Credits are the universal currency for features that have a direct operational cost. This ensures you only pay for what you use.

  • What are credits for? Accessing powerful, third-party AI models via OpenRouter.
  • How do I get credits? Purchase them directly via PayHip or get a monthly stipend by subscribing on Patreon.
  • Pricing: 100 credits = $1.00 USD.

The Subscription Tiers

Subscriptions are the best way to get the most out of Alfred, offering increased access to our built-in features.

  • What are subscriptions for? Unlocking higher daily limits on features like Deep Research, Image Generation, and Image Upscaling.
  • Bonus Credits: Subscriptions also grant you a monthly bonus drop of credits as a thank you!
  • Exclusive Perks: The Pro tier unlocks exclusive features like Custom AI Personalities.

Purchasing Credits

Topping up your balance is easy. All purchases are handled securely through PayHip.

Pay-As-You-Go (PAYG)

Perfect for trying out premium models or for infrequent use. You can top up your credit balance at any time.

Please Note: A 5% transaction fee is applied to all purchases to cover payment processing costs. For example, a $10.00 purchase will add 950 credits ($9.50) to your account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are there two systems (Credits and Subscriptions)? +

This hybrid model offers the most flexibility.
• Credits are for features with direct, variable costs (like expensive AI models), so you only pay for what you use.
• Subscriptions are for unlocking higher volumes of our built-in features (like Deep Research and Image Generation) that have fixed or very low operational costs for us.

Do my subscription's monthly credits roll over? +

No, the monthly credit stipend from a Patreon subscription does not roll over. It is refreshed at the start of each billing cycle. This encourages active use and allows us to offer them at a great value.

Do purchased credits expire? +

No, credits you purchase directly never expire. They will remain in your account until you use them.

Can I use my subscription's monthly credits on any feature? +

Yes! The credits you receive from your subscription are added to your main credit balance and can be used for any feature that requires credits, such as OpenRouter models.
