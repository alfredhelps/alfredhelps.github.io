Features

Discover What Alfred Can Do For You

AI Capabilities

Multiple AI Models

Alfred offers a variety of AI models to suit your needs:

  • Default Model - Optimized for general assistance and everyday tasks
  • Advanced Models - Enhanced capabilities including vision support
  • Specialized Models - Tailored for specific use cases and conversation styles

Custom AI Personalities

Create and use personalized AI assistants tailored to your specific needs. Define their tone, knowledge areas, and behavior to get the perfect assistant for any task.

Image Generation & Analysis

Generate stunning images from text descriptions using advanced AI. Alfred can also analyze images, providing detailed descriptions and insights about visual content.

Smart Web Search

Get real-time information from the web without leaving Discord. Alfred can search the internet and provide up-to-date answers to your questions.

Context-Aware Conversations

Alfred remembers up to 140 messages per channel, allowing for natural, flowing conversations that maintain context over time.

Vision Support

Send images to Alfred for analysis and include them as context in your conversations. Perfect for getting information about visual content or using images as reference.

Command System

Core Commands

  • /ask - Ask a question to the AI assistant
  • /user-settings - Configure your AI preferences
  • /imagine - Generate AI images from text descriptions

Conversation Management

  • /delete-history - Clear conversation history
  • /export-history - Export chat logs for backup

Server Settings

  • /create-personality - Create custom AI personalities
  • /server-settings - Configure server-wide bot behavior

Utility Commands

  • /about - Display bot information and statistics
  • /ping - Check bot latency and performance
  • /notifications - View your notifications and updates
  • /how-to-use - Detailed help guide for using Alfred
