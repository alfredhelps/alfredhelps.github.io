Thank You For Your Purchase!

Your support helps keep Alfred running. Follow the steps below to claim your credits.

Your Credit Code

Please check the email you used to make the purchase. You will find your unique Credit Code (it may be called a "License Key" in the email).

How to Claim

Once you have your code, go to any channel where Alfred is present in Discord and use the following command:

/claim-credits <your-credit-code>

Your credits will be added to your account instantly!
